About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology since Spring 2022, advised by Dr. Kai Shu. I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from the Chongqing University in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xie. My research interests generally lie in data mining and machine learning, including graph learning, time-series forecasting and multi-armed bandit with their applications in anomaly detection, HPC system and recommender system.
