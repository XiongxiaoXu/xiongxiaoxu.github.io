About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology since Spring 2022, advised by Dr. Kai Shu. My research interests generally lie in machine learning, particularly in multimodal LLMs and time series analysis (e.g., forecasting, anomaly detection). I also have research experiences on GNNs, multi-armed bandits (RL), and their applications in anomaly detection, recommender system, and HPC.

Previously, I worked as a research intern at Argonne Leadership Computing Facility, Argonne National Laboratory, Summer 2024. I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from the Chongqing University in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xie.
