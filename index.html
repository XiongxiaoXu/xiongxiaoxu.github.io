About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology since Spring 2022, advised by Dr. Kai Shu. I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from the Chongqing University in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xie. I worked as a research intern at Argonne Leadership Computing Facility, Argonne National Laboratory. My research interests generally lie in data mining and machine learning, and their applications in high-performance computing. Previously, I work on multi-armed bandits and its application in recommender system.
